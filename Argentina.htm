ARGENTINA

The Economy

Through this program students will learn all about the current economic conditions in Argentina. Argentina used to be classified as an Emerging Economy but after economic crisis’, the country is moving back towards a Frontier Market as their economy begins to slow down. Students will learn about how the Argentine economy got to the place it did today and see the impact of how it affects the everyday lives of those living in Argentina. Students will be working with Argentine economist and local businesses, as well as living in a home with an Argentine family. Students are encouraged to ask questions about the current conditions in order to get a grasp of what is currently going on.
