What is this website about?

This website is about my time studying abroad in Argentina, Chile, and Peru.

What is the objective of this website?

The objective of this website is to help students who may be considering study abroad in South America get a better picture of what it is like. It also can help them find different things they would be interested in doing and places that they could travel to while they are there. This is important becauswe a lot of people don't know much about traveling through South America or think about studying abroad there.

The Target Audience

The target audience is college students who are deciding where to study abroad. It also can be for people who are simply trying to travel to South America.
